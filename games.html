Games

A space dedicated to the games I play and enjoy.

Analog

Chess

1. e4 enjoyer and 1. d4 hater. Huge fan of Tal and Fischer. I typically play bullet games online since improving in chess is overrated. There's nothing quite like finding a beautiful tactic under time pressure.

Table Tennis

It requires very little physical effort, which makes it an obvious sport for me to pick up. Once you start landing your Snake spins, it quickly becomes surprisingly addictive.

Clash Royale

Look, how could I not play the most generic mobile game out there? I proudly play it decently well, so I'm always up for some friendly (or sweaty) competition if you want to challenge me.

Osu!

I recently started clicking circles to the beat. Right now, I am just profoundly impressed by how genuinely terrible I am at it. It's a humbling experience.
